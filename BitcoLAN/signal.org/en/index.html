Why use Bitorzo?

Explore below to see why Bitorzo is a simple, powerful, and secure messenger

Share Without Insecurity

State-of-the-art end-to-end encryption (powered by the open source Bitorzo Protocol) keeps your Bitcoin secure. We can't read your Private Key or Transaction info , and no one else can either. Privacy isn’t an optional mode — it’s just the way that Bitorzo works. Every Transaction, every time.

No Down Times

Send Bitcoin Easily as a messaging , Bitorzo is secure & fast wallet for free. Bitorzo uses your phone's data connection so you can avoid BTC fees.

Safe

Make crystal-clear BTC Transaction to people who live across town, or across the ocean, with no long-distance charges.

Instant On

Bitorzo is fast, because it uses servers that index the Bitcoin blockchain..

Multisig

Split the permission to spend your coins between several wallets.

No ads. No trackers. No kidding.

There are no ads, no affiliate marketers, and no creepy tracking in Bitorzo. So focus on sharing the moments that matter with the people who matter to you.

Free for Everyone

Bitorzo is an independent nonprofit. We're not tied to any major tech companies, and we can never be acquired by one either. Development is supported by grants and donations from people like you.
